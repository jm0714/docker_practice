Cats Facts

  • Cats have retractable claws.
  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
  • Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  • Cats sleep for an average of 12-16 hours a day.
  • Cats have whiskers on the backs of their front legs too.
  • A cat's nose print is unique, like a human's fingerprint.
  • Cats can jump up to six times their body length in one leap.
  • Cats have a strong territorial instinct.
  • Cats' whiskers are highly sensitive and help them navigate in the dark.
  • Cats can make over 100 different sounds.
